How can I unfreeze my account?

Generally, a membership freeze is set for a pre-determined amount of time. As such, the freeze will automatically end at the designated time, at which point your account will reactivate and your membership dues billing will resume as usual. If you would like to end the freeze earlier, please contact your local club, or email us to submit your reactivation request.

Can I put my membership on hold?

You may request a one-month courtesy freeze if you are a Monthly Payment member.

Please note that your membership must be active and you must be a member in good standing to be placed on freeze.

Extended freezes may be available if one of the following factors apply:

  • Medical – a temporary medical situation which renders you unable to use the academy for a specified amount of time.
  • Work Transfer – a temporary out-of-area work transfer that takes you more than 50 miles from ATOS HQ SAN DIEGO, CA facility.
  • Volunteer Work – a temporary volunteer work assignment that takes you more than 50 miles from ATOS HQ SAN DIEGO, CA facility.
  • Military Deployment – deployment to a location more than 50 miles from ATOS HQ SAN DIEGO, CA facility.

To place your membership on freeze, contact us or send your request to staff@atosjiujitsuhq.com. We reserve the right to modify, change or cancel the freeze options available to members at any time without prior notice, at our sole discretion.
